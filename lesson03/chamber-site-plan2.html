Dominic Abah

WDD 230

Overview

Purpose

To bring businesses together to take advantage of economies of scale and grow bigger and better than ever envisioned

To expose the businesses, traders and farmers within the Benue South-West vicinity the current best practices in commerce

Audience

Businesses, traders and farmers within the South-Wast of Benue state who are finding individual survival difficult or looking to take advantage of best practices in commerce

Scenarios

  • What do I stand to benefit from this site?
  • Is this chamber better than any other one in the neighboring community?
  • How seasoned are the board of directors of this chamber in commerce?
  • Why are there no link to the personal sites of the directors to enable us know them better?
